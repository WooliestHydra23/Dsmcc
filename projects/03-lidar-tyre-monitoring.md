# LiDAR-Based Tyre Monitoring System

## Overview
Researched and documented a product concept for an IoT-enabled vehicle safety system using LiDAR to monitor tyre condition in real-time.

## Problem Statement
Tyre failure is a significant cause of accidents and fleet downtime. How could emerging technology (LiDAR) solve this problem with a product approach?

## My Role & Contributions
- Researched LiDAR technology and its applications in transportation
- Analyzed market gap and customer pain points (commercial vehicles, logistics)
- Documented product concept and value proposition
- Created feature roadmap and use case scenarios
- Designed presentation and pitch materials

## Process & Approach
- Researched LiDAR sensors, costs, and technical feasibility
- Interviewed logistics and transportation experts (secondary research)
- Mapped customer decision-making (fleet managers, safety teams)
- Identified key product features and differentiators
- Built product concept document and pitch deck

## Tools Used
- Google Sheets (market data)
- Canva (product visuals and diagrams)
- PowerPoint (presentation)

## Key Outputs
- Product concept document (problem, solution, target market)
- Feature roadmap and technical specifications overview
- Customer use case scenarios
- Market opportunity sizing
- Pitch presentation and supporting visuals

## Skills Demonstrated
- Product innovation and concept development
- Problem framing and research
- Customer understanding and use case mapping
- Technical product communication
- Presentation design

## Impact & Learnings
Explored how emerging technology can be applied to real-world safety problems. Learned that product success depends on clearly articulating customer pain points, technology feasibility, and market readiness — not just the technology itself.
